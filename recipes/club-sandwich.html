Club Sandwich

HOME

Description

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chicken
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mayo
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Lightly toast your bread
  2. Cook your protein
  3. Slice up your veggies
  4. Apply it all on your bread in different layers
  5. Dont forget to add the mayo
  6. Slice it up in triangles and secure each piece with a toothpick
